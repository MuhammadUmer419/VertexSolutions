Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services.

Do you provide both Frontend and Backend development?

Yes! We build complete solutions including modern Frontend (React, Vue, Angular) and secure Backend (Laravel, Node.js, PHP, Python) depending on project requirements.

Can you develop Mobile Applications?

We develop cross-platform mobile apps using Flutter and React Native as well as native Android/iOS apps to meet your specific business needs.

Do you offer WordPress or CMS-based websites?

Yes, we provide fast and professional WordPress, Shopify, and custom CMS solutions for blogs, eCommerce stores, and business websites.

What about website maintenance and support?

We offer ongoing support and maintenance services including updates, security monitoring, bug fixes, and new feature development.

How long does it take to complete a project?

Project timelines depend on complexity. A basic website may take 1–2 weeks, while full-stack apps or mobile apps usually take 4–8 weeks.

Do you provide custom pricing for startups?

Yes, we offer flexible pricing and special packages for startups, ensuring affordability without compromising quality.
